About MindfulMoments

MindfulMoments is a privacy-focused mental health tracking app designed to help you understand patterns in your wellbeing. By consistently checking in with yourself, you can discover what impacts your mood, energy, and overall mental health.

✨ Features

  • Daily mood and energy tracking
  • Sleep quality monitoring
  • Factor tracking (exercise, stress, social time, etc.)
  • Personal journaling
  • Visual trend analysis
  • Pattern-based insights
  • Data export capability

🔒 Privacy First

Your data is stored locally in your browser only. Nothing is ever sent to any server. You have complete ownership and can export or delete your data at any time.

💡 Tips for Success

  • Check in at the same time each day for consistency
  • Be honest with yourself - this is for you, not anyone else
  • Look for patterns after 2-3 weeks of tracking
  • Use the notes field to capture context and insights
  • Remember: mental health is a journey, not a destination
